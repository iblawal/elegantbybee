Discovery Call

Our Discovery Call is where we get to know you — your story, your vision, and your expectations. We ask smart questions and listen intentionally to help shape the blueprint of your celebration. Whether you're sure of your theme or just have a feeling, we translate that into strategy and direction that starts the journey right.

Why It Matters

Most planners start with a quote. We start with a conversation. Our discovery call allows us to understand not only your logistics, but also your values, your lifestyle, and your emotional goals for the event. It's where we uncover the *why* behind your celebration.

What You’ll Walk Away With

  • ✔️ A clear outline of your goals and event type
  • ✔️ Recommendations based on your personality, not just your budget
  • ✔️ Confidence that your planner sees you clearly
